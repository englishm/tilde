Screening ASCII Star Wars

NB: This is not very (read: not) secure. As always, please don't hack the Gibson.

As the host:

tmux -S /tmp/starwars new -s starwars
chmod 777 /tmp/starwars

Then wall to let people know it's ready and they can join with:

tmux -S /tmp/starwars attach -t starwars

Once people have joined, start the film:

telnet towel.blinkenlights.nl

Enjoy! (It ends pretty early, so don't be disappointed.)

Cleanup

You can clean up afterwards by removing the shared socket:

rm /tmp/starwars
